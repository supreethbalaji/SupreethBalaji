About me

Why I am a candidate for Regional Youth Representative?

My scouting experience has taught me values, provided me with many opportunities to make new friends and the adventures are endless, and now, I want to give back to the organization that gave so much and more. I would love to serve as the Regional Youth Representative because I strongly believe that most important part of scouting and scout method is to create possibilities and build bridges among the NSOs to exchange ideas and also implement scouting in the new normal by keeping the in the trends of youth like scouting with STEM, implementation of common UN goals through scouts for SDGs and extend inclusion for more young people with special needs. You probably don't know me well or not know me at all, but I do promise you that I will be a very dedicated RYR and someone who believes that a variety of viewpoints of young people should be heard and I also commit to add value to this team.

What value I bring at the table as an RYR and to the Asia-Pacific Region?

"One World, One Promise" We are the largest uniformed youth movement with a lot of diversity, so we need to take advantage of the opportunities offered within the new normal environment of scouting. Being one of the most diverse regions in the world of Scouting. We, along with the elements of our regional triennium, must have a constant dialogue among the youth of our region. If elected as RYR, I would bring my expertise in teamwork and strategic leadership to connect and ensure the youth of our region with huge opportunities. The movement has given me a chance to learn new skills and inspire me to strive for greater heights, and this experience as a Scout is once in a lifetime,and I personally believe that all Young People should experience Scouting. The Scouting values were a key part of my success story of building my confidence as I developed my startup with my expertise in STEM and entrepreneurship.
